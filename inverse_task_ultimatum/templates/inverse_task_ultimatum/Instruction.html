Summary of this experiment.
This experiment involves many participants. Each participant is paired anonymously with another participant. You will have to make a decision and your partner will have to make a different decision.
There is an allocation of 10 points to be divided between you and your paired participant. Points will be given to you based on your and your paired participant’s decisions.
Your and your paired participant’s earnings could be zero. Hence, a showing fee of 3 euros will be given to you at any case. Conversion rate 1 point = 1 Euro
Procedures.
Main Instructions:
  • Randomly and anonymously you will be paired with another participant.
  • 10 points is intended for both of you.
  • You could be the first mover (A) and your paired participant the second mover (B), or the opposite.
Roles:
  • (A) decides how to divide the 10 points between (A) and (B)
  • if (A) wants to claim more points for her/himself (A) has to exert more amount of effort.
  • (B) decides whether to accept or reject (A)’s decision.
  • If (B) accepts (A)’s decision, both (A) and (B) will earn points according to (A)’s decision.
  • If (B) rejects (A)’s decision, both (A) and (B) will get zero.
  • (A) will be informed of (B)’s decision.
￼Note:
If you fully understand the procedures above you can click now the button ‘next’ to be randomly assigned to one of the two roles (A) or (B). If you have any question, raise your hand before clicking ‘start’ button and the instructor will answer your question.
